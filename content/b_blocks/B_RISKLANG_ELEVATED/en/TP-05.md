# Section 10: Risk Factors

Based on your responses, factors have been identified that may influence treatment outcomes. This is important to understand.

Elevated risk factors require careful consideration:
- They may affect which treatments are advisable
- They could influence healing and long-term success
- Additional precautions or modifications may be needed
- Closer monitoring might be recommended

Your dentist will need to:
- Assess how these factors apply to your specific case
- Discuss realistic expectations
- Determine appropriate approaches
- Plan for potential complications

To support the best possible outcome:
- Maintain rigorous oral hygiene
- Follow all instructions precisely
- Attend every follow-up appointment
- Inform your dentist promptly if issues arise

Discuss these factors honestly with your dentist. Full information enables appropriate planning.
