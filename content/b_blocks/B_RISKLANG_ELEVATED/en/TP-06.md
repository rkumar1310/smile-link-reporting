# Section 10: Risk Factors

Based on your responses, factors have been identified that are relevant to treatment planning. It's important that you and your dentist discuss these.

Elevated risk factors mean:
- Treatment planning may require additional consideration
- Some approaches might be modified for your situation
- Closer monitoring may be beneficial
- Managing these factors becomes part of your care plan

Your dentist can explain:
- How these factors specifically affect your options
- What can be done to address them
- What you can expect given your situation

Factors within your control that support good outcomes:
- Maintaining excellent oral hygiene
- Following care instructions
- Keeping scheduled appointments
- Communicating openly with your dental team

Having this information allows you to make informed decisions about your care.
