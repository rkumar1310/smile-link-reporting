# Section 10: Risk Factors

Uw antwoorden geven aan factors that warrant consideration in treatment planning. These require disclosure to your treating dentist.

Elevated risk factors influence but do not preclude treatment. Clinical management includes:
- Assessing factor-specific implications
- Implementing risk mitigation protocols
- Adapting treatment approaches as indicated
- Establishing appropriate monitoring schedules

Patient-modifiable factors supporting outcomes:
- Rigorous oral hygiene maintenance
- Strict protocol adherence
- Consistent follow-up attendance
- Healthcare provider coordination when applicable

Complete disclosure of relevant factors enables optimal treatment planning and risk management.
