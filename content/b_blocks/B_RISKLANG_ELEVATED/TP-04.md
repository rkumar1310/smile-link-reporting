# Section 10: Risk Factors

Your responses have identified factors that require attention in treatment planning. This is important information, and being aware of it actually helps achieve better outcomes.

Many patients with elevated risk factors receive successful treatment. The key is proper planning. Your dentist will:
- Assess how these factors specifically affect your situation
- Discuss proven strategies for managing these risks
- Adjust treatment approaches where beneficial
- Set up appropriate follow-up schedules

You can actively contribute to good outcomes by:
- Maintaining excellent oral hygiene
- Following pre- and post-treatment instructions carefully
- Attending all follow-up appointments
- Keeping communication open between your healthcare providers

Being aware of these factors and addressing them proactively gives you the best foundation for successful treatment.
