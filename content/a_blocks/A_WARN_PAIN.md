# Important Notice: Pain Symptoms

You have indicated that you are experiencing pain or discomfort. Pain can be a sign of conditions that require prompt attention.

**We recommend:**
- Scheduling a dental consultation as soon as reasonably possible
- Not delaying if the pain is severe, increasing, or accompanied by swelling
- Contacting a dental professional if you experience sudden worsening

This report provides general information, but pain symptoms should always be evaluated by a dental professional who can examine your specific situation.

*If you are experiencing severe pain, swelling, or signs of infection, please seek immediate dental care.*
