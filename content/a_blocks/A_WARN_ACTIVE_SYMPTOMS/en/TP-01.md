# Important Notice: Active Symptoms

You have indicated experiencing pain or discomfort. This requires attention before proceeding with elective treatment planning.

**Please note:**
- Active symptoms should be evaluated by a dentist promptly
- Pain may indicate an underlying condition requiring treatment
- The priority is addressing your current discomfort

Your dentist will assess your symptoms and determine appropriate next steps. Treatment planning for restorations or other procedures typically follows once acute issues are resolved.
