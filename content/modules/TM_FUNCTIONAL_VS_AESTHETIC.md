# Functional vs. Aesthetic Balance

Because your situation may contain both functional and aesthetic aspects, it is important to clarify priorities. Many people are uncertain about what should be addressed first.

This influences the order and focus of the treatment trajectory. This does not mean that one aspect is ignored, but rather that choices are logically ordered.

Explicitly discuss this with your dentist, so that the approach aligns with what you find most important.
