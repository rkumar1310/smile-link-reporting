# Oral Complexity

When multiple factors come together, we speak of oral complexity. Many people experience confusion or uncertainty about the trajectory because of this.

This influences how treatments are planned and phased. This does not mean that solutions are limited, but rather that extra coordination is needed.

Explicitly discuss this with your dentist, so that expectations and planning align well.
