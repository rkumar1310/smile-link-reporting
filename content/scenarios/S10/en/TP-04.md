# Section 2: Personal Summary

You've learned that most of your teeth cannot be saved. While this may feel significant, it's important to know that this situation has well-established, highly successful treatment options. The path forward is clear and proven.

# Section 3: Your Situation

Learning that comprehensive treatment is needed can feel overwhelming at first. But this is actually a very well-understood situation in dental medicine. Full-mouth rehabilitation has been performed successfully for decades, with excellent outcomes.

Your situation is not unusual—it's exactly what these treatments are designed for. Modern dental solutions can fully restore function, comfort, and appearance. The procedures are well-documented, predictable, and performed routinely by specialists.

# Section 4: Treatment Directions

Three proven approaches exist for full-mouth restoration. All have strong success rates and clear outcomes.

# Section 5: Option 1 — Fixed Teeth Upper and Lower

**Why this is reliable**
- Gold standard for full restoration
- Highest patient satisfaction rates
- Decades of clinical evidence
- Predictable, long-lasting results

# Section 6: Option 2 — Snap-on Dentures Upper and Lower

**Why this is reliable**
- Well-established improvement over traditional dentures
- Proven approach with good outcomes
- Clear, predictable procedure
- Good long-term stability

# Section 7: Option 3 — Fixed Upper + Snap-on Lower

**Why this is reliable**
- Proven combination approach
- Well-documented outcomes
- Balanced solution
- Routinely performed

# Section 8-11: [Practical Details]

Duration: 4-9 months | Recovery: Manageable, 5-10 days per phase | Cost: €8,000-€50,000
