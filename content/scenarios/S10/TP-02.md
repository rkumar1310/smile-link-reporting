# Section 2: Personal Summary

You've learned that most of your teeth can no longer be saved. We understand this can feel like a significant moment—perhaps even overwhelming. You deserve a solution that restores not just function, but your confidence and quality of life.

# Section 3: Your Situation

Receiving news that most of your teeth cannot be preserved is difficult. Many people describe it as a turning point—the realization that maintaining what's left is no longer realistic, and that something more comprehensive is needed.

You may have been dealing with discomfort, uncertainty, or difficulty eating for some time. The current situation may feel unstable, and daily activities that should be simple—like enjoying a meal—might require more effort than they should.

What's important to know is that this is not an ending—it's a beginning. Modern dental solutions can restore full function, comfort, and confidence. Many people in your situation have found that comprehensive treatment transformed their daily lives in ways they didn't expect.

# Section 4: Treatment Directions

When most teeth cannot be saved, several full-mouth approaches offer meaningful restoration. Each represents a real path to renewed comfort and function.

# Section 5: Option 1 — Fixed Teeth Upper and Lower

**What others have valued**

- No removal or adjustment needed

- Restored confidence in every interaction

# Section 6: Option 2 — Snap-on Dentures Upper and Lower

**What others have valued**
