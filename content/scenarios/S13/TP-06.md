# Section 2: Personal Summary

You're young with light tooth discoloration. Treatment options exist. Whether to pursue them, and which to choose, is entirely your decision.

# Section 3: Your Situation

Light discoloration in healthy teeth is an aesthetic concern. Some people find it significant; others are unbothered. How much it matters is personal.

There is no medical need to treat discoloration. If you choose to address it, options are available. The decision is yours.

# Section 4: Treatment Options

**Option 1 — Professional Whitening**
- Brightens natural color
- Reversible approach
- No permanent changes

**Option 2 — Veneers**
- More control over final appearance
- Longer-lasting results
- More significant decision
