# Section 2: Personal Summary

You're young, your teeth are healthy, but you've noticed they're not as bright as you'd like them to be. We understand that even subtle discoloration can affect how confident you feel about your smile.

# Section 3: Your Situation

When you're young and notice your teeth look a bit dull or less white than you'd like, it can be surprisingly bothersome—even if others might not notice. You might see it in photos, under certain lighting, or when comparing yourself to others.

The good news is that your teeth are healthy and present. This means any improvement can be approached gently, without major interventions. Many people in your situation just want to "freshen up" their smile a bit—nothing dramatic, just enough to feel more confident.

# Section 4: Treatment Directions

Two gentle approaches can address light discoloration. One brightens your natural teeth; the other offers more control over the final look.

# Section 5: Option 1 — Professional Teeth Whitening

**What others have valued**

- Natural-looking results
- Easy to maintain

# Section 6: Option 2 — Veneers

**What others have valued**
