# Section 2: Personal Summary

Your old dental work is showing its age—and we understand that can be frustrating. The good news is that modern materials and techniques can restore a more natural, uniform appearance.

# Section 3: Your Situation

Dental work doesn't last forever. Over time, materials darken, margins become visible, and what once blended seamlessly may now stand out. It's not uncommon to feel self-conscious about restorations that have aged.

Many people in your situation remember when their dental work looked fine—and wonder why it's suddenly noticeable. The answer is usually simple: materials age, and standards evolve. What was excellent work decades ago may no longer meet today's aesthetic expectations.

The opportunity here is to update your smile with modern materials that will look better and likely last longer.

# Section 4: Treatment Directions

Replacement of old restorations with modern materials can significantly improve your smile's appearance.

**Crown Replacement** eliminates visible metal and outdated materials.
**Filling Replacement** removes dark or discolored restorations.
