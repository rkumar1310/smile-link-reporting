# Section 2: Personal Summary

You have old dental restorations that could be updated. Options exist. Whether to pursue them is your choice.

# Section 3: Your Situation

Old dental work may function fine but look dated. Some people prefer to update; others are satisfied as is. Both choices are valid.

If restorations are failing functionally, that's a different matter. If the concern is purely aesthetic, it becomes a personal decision.

# Section 4: Treatment Options

Modern materials can replace old crowns and fillings with more natural-looking alternatives.

**What this offers you**
- Improved aesthetics
- Updated materials
- Modern techniques

**What to consider**
- Cost per restoration
- Time investment
- May not be necessary for function

# Section 5-11: [Practical Information]

Standard information for restorative procedures.

**Your next steps are entirely up to you.** Unless restorations are failing, this is an elective decision. Take whatever time you need.
